Algorithm
Step by step instructions to solve a problem.
Debug
Find and fix mistakes in a computer program.
Input
Data that goes into a computer.
Process
Something that happens to change data into useful information.
Output
Information that comes out of a computer.
Program
A list of instructions that tells a computer exactly what to do.
Sequence
More than one instruction to be followed in order.
Selection
When your program has to make a choice.
Repetition
Doing the same instruction more than once.
Variable
Something your program needs to remember that might change.
